OAP Contest Manager — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-08

OAP Contest Manager ("the app") is a free tool that helps UIL One-Act Play contest managers generate contest documents. This policy explains what data the app handles and why.

What we collect

  • Account identity. When you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address to identify your account. When you sign in with an emailed link, we store your email address. We do not receive or store any password.
  • Your contest data. The contest information you enter (schools, directors, plays, dates, adjudicators, and related details) is stored so you can return to it across devices. It is associated only with your account and is never sold or shared.

What stays on your device

Speechwire credentials you enter are stored only in your browser on your device. They are never transmitted to or stored on our server. Document generation also happens entirely in your browser.

How we use it

Account identity is used solely to authenticate you and keep your contests separate from other users'. Contest data is used solely to provide the app's functionality (saving, syncing, and generating your documents). We do not use your data for advertising and do not sell it.

Data retention and deletion

Your contest data is retained while your account is active. To delete your account and its data, contact us at the address below.
